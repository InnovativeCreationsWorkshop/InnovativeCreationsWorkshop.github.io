About Us

Interactive Creations Workshop specializes in designing and developing immersive interactive experiences using cutting-edge technologies like Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR), and Mixed Reality (MR). With a strong focus on user experience and creative design, we bring visionary ideas to life in virtual and interactive spaces.

Our expertise lies in blending art, design, and technology to craft unique and engaging digital experiences. Whether it's interactive holograms, projection mapping, or immersive storytelling, we are committed to pushing the boundaries of innovation.

At Interactive Creations Workshop, we not only create but also consult and educate, helping individuals and businesses navigate the evolving world of immersive media. Our mission is to empower creators, brands, and organizations to transform their ideas into dynamic, interactive realities.

Meet the Founder

Tina M is a passionate artist, designer, and technologist with over a decade of experience in interactive immersive media, design, and development. With a BFA in Digital Media, a minor in Creative Writing, and an MFA in Media Arts specializing in creative technologies, she blends artistry with innovation to craft cutting-edge interactive experiences.

As the founder of Interactive Creations Workshop, Tina is dedicated to helping visionaries bring their ideas to life through immersive technologies like VR, AR, and mixed reality. She thrives on problem-solving, designing user-centered solutions, and exploring emerging technologies like interactive holograms and projection-based experiences. Beyond development, she enjoys consulting, teaching, and empowering others to achieve their creative and technological goals.
