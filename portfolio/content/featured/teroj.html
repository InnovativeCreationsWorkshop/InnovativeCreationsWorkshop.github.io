T'Roj VR

Software Used: Unreal Engine, Houdini

I collaborated with Motion Machina Studio on a creative project that brings to life a world of mystery and magic. My contributions included 3D modeling and animation of floating cards, a flying phoenix, and the Zoltar box, along with the design of the cards and other visual elements. I also provided VR consulting focused on user experience and immersive environment design.
