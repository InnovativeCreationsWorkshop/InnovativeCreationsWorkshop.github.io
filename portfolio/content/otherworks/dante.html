Dante's Inferno

Motion Graphics: Kinetic Type Study

Software Used: AfterEffects

This project explored the art of kinetic typography through motion graphics, with a focus on bringing literary lines to life in dynamic, engaging ways. Using powerful excerpts from Dante Alighieri’s Inferno, the goal was to visually represent the intensity and drama of the text through movement, typography, and visual effects. The exercise involved timing the text animations to enhance the emotional impact of Dante’s words, creating a visual experience that complements the themes of the work.
