Pink's Donut Co.

Mock Ad

Software Used: Houdini, TouchDesigner, AfterEffects

Pink’s Donut Co. is a whimsical, conceptual brand that specializes in crafting fresh, organic donuts from scratch. In this motion graphics project, I set out to explore the creative possibilities of combining 3D modeling and real-time rendering with a focus on dynamic visual storytelling.

To begin, I experimented with Houdini for modeling the donuts and developed a unique workflow that integrated TouchDesigner for real-time rendering. The first phase of the project involved modeling the donuts in Houdini, then exporting them as FBX files into TouchDesigner. There, I used CHOP (Channel Operator) data to drive a playful "donut rain" effect, where hundreds of donuts fall from the sky in a captivating cascade.

After successfully rendering the real-time donut rain, I switched to Houdini once again to simulate a donut drop, where donuts collide with a flat surface. This sequence was rendered using Mantra, Houdini’s powerful rendering system. Once all the elements were rendered, I composited the final scene in After Effects, focusing heavily on color correction, grading, and enhancing realism by adding shadows and fine details.
