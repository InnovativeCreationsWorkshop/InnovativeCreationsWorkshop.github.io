Breathless

A Mock Trailer

Software Used: AfterEffects

What if a French New Wave film was recreated as a Hong Kong Film? Using Jean Luc-Godard's Breathless to recreate as a crime fighting action pack Jackie Chan Film. This was the concept for this trailer. Working with Jon Yousef painted every frame for this piece.
