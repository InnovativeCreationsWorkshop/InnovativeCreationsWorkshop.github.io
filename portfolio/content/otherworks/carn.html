Carnival of Souls

A VFX Study: Green Screen and Add In

Software Used: NukeX

This project focused on integrating a new character seamlessly into a classic film scene using green screen techniques. The objective was to match the texture, lighting, and overall visual quality of the original footage to create a cohesive and believable composite. The source material for this exercise was Carnival of Souls (1962), directed by Herk Harvey.
