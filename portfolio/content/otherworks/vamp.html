Vampire Throne

Software Used: Unreal Engine, Autodesk Maya, Marmoset, Quixel Suite, Photoshop

This project is a conceptual piece inspired by a narrative I developed about a vampire queen. It serves as an exercise in designing a game-ready environment within Unreal Engine, where I focused on mastering techniques for creating textures and implementing custom shaders.
