Packaging Options

We offer various packing options, including bales, capsacks, and boxes. Should you have specific packing requirements, we're happy to accommodate them. The total weights mentioned are for a 40 ft. HQ container, although our minimum order quantity (MOQ) is a 20 ft. container.

Clothing / H.H.R

Mixed Clothing is packed into 1,000 lb. bales, while Graded Clothing comes in 100 lb. bales.

Credential

Available loose or in gaylord boxes, approximately yielding 35,000 lbs. Also offered in 400-500 lb. bales, yielding around 44,000 lbs.

Shoes

A&B grades come in 50 lb. or 450 lb. options, totaling 40,000 lbs. Single shoes are packed into 450 lb. capsacks, also totaling 40,000 lbs.

Soft Toys

Graded soft toys come in 300 lb. capsacks, totaling 25,000 lbs.

Suitecases

Stacked and shrink-wrapped onto pallets. Each pallet, weighing between 500 - 800 lbs., totals 20,000 lbs. Travel/laptop bags, purses, and backpacks are nested inside to maximize space.
