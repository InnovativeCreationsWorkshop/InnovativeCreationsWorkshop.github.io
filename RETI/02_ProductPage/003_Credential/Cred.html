Credential

Whether you're replenishing your thrift store inventory or appealing to your customer base, our Credential offering stands as an exceptional option. Sourced directly from donation bins, our goods remain untouched, ensuring authenticity. Within our collection, you'll discover a diverse array of items, including clothing, shoes, accessories, housewares, and more. With donations sourced exclusively from Southern California, you can rely on consistent quality throughout. Our Credential bales are meticulously wrapped with fabric and cardboard for optimum protection, yielding up to 44,000 lbs. per shipment. Alternatively, if packed loosely in large boxes, we can accommodate up to 35,000 lbs. per 40 ft. HQ container. Due to its popularity, availability may be limited, so act swiftly to secure your order!
