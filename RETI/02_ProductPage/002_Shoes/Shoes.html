Shoes

Discover our diverse range of footwear options, catering to every member of the family. From men's and women's shoes to children's footwear, our collections boast quality and durability. Whether you're seeking individual pairs or mixed assortments, our products promise style, comfort, and reliability.

Paired Shoes

Our top-selling shoe combination features A & B pairs, catering to men, women, and children alike. These shoes are in excellent, reusable condition. You can choose between 50 or 450 lb. capsacks, and we can load up to 40,000 lbs. per 40 ft. HQ container. Additionally, we offer unsorted shoes packed into gaylord boxes. These shoes are packed as they come, ensuring you receive a varied assortment of used shoes, totaling approximately 30,000 lbs. per 40 ft. HQ container.

Single Shoes

Our Single Shoes collection comprises men's, women's, and children's footwear in excellent, reusable condition. This selection excludes rollerskates, slippers, or high heels over 3 inches. There's a minimal inclusion of high boots. These shoes are packed into 450 lb. capsacks, allowing for up to 40,000 lbs. per 40 ft. HQ container.
