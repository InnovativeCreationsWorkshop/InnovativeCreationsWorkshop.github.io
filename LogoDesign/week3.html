Week 3: Color Theory and Typography

Presentation

Assignment

  • Create a vector-based logo using Adobe Illustrator: Utilize Adobe Illustrator's tools to design a scalable logo with crisp lines and shapes, ensuring it retains quality when resized.
  • Develop a mockup using a template: Select a suitable mockup template that aligns with your project's needs, such as a stationary mockup for branding or a product mockup for packaging design. Customize the template by inserting your logo and adjusting elements to visualize how the design will appear in real-world scenarios.
  • Prepare for your presentation: Organize your materials, such as slides or a digital portfolio, to effectively communicate your design concept, process, and rationale. Practice articulating key points and be ready to address questions or feedback from stakeholders.
