Graphic Design - Logo Design

Introduction to Logo Design

Course Overview

Each class will consist of a mix of lectures, practical exercises, and design critiques.

Course Description

In this class, participants will immerse themselves in the world of logo design, uncovering the influence of visual identity and branding. They will gain knowledge on crafting an exemplary logo design brief, a comprehensive document that equips graphic designers with all the essential details to craft a logo tailored to your enterprise. Furthermore, students will acquire the skills to conceptualize, craft, and perfect logos that effectively communicate the core identity and principles of a brand.

Learning Objectives

By the end of the course, students will be able to:

  • Understand the fundamental principles of logo design.
  • Apply design software to bring logo concepts to life.
  • Critically assess and revise logo designs based on feedback.

Course Content and Topics

  • Week 1: Fundamentals of Logo Design
  • Week 2: Conceptualizing and Sketching Logos
  • Week 3: Digital Logo Design (Using Adobe Illustrator or similar software)
  • Week 4: Logo Refinement and Presentation

Teaching Methods and Strategies

  • Each class will consist of a mix of lectures, practical exercises, and design critiques.
  • Students will work on individual logo design projects and participate in group discussions.

Assessment and Evaluation

  • Assessment will be based on the quality of the logo designs created during the course.
  • Evaluation will include peer reviews and instructor feedback.

Textbooks and Learning Materials

  • Adobe Illustrator (or alternative design software)
  • Drawing software (Photoshop, Photopeia, Procreate)
