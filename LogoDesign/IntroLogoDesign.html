Course Content - Logo Design

Week 1
Fundamentals of Logo Design

Explore the power of logos in shaping brand identity through an insightful journey. Learn logo design basics and branding briefs, tracing their historical evolution. Familiarize yourself with Adobe Illustrator and essential design principles. Discover how to create impactful logos, emphasizing simplicity, versatility, and memorability, through theoretical learning and practical exercises.

Week 2
Principles of Design and Creative Process

Embark on a creative journey exploring design principles like balance and contrast, crucial in logo creation. Witness concept evolution from ideation to refinement through sketching and brainstorming. Each stroke on paper signifies potential brand identities. Refinement merges creativity and strategy, crafting captivating logos that resonate with audiences. Join this exhilarating voyage where design principles illuminate the path to timeless logo creation.

Week 3
Basic Color Theory + Typography_______

Explore typography and fonts, understanding their nuances and impact. Learn to merge them seamlessly with graphical elements for compelling logos. Discover best practices for incorporating text effectively. Delve into color psychology, mastering strategic color selection to evoke desired emotions. Engage in hands-on exercises to refine color skills. Join this immersive journey where typefaces, fonts, and colors converge to shape memorable logos.

Week 4
Logo Brief Presentation and Critique_____

Present your logo designs with finesse, guided by comprehensive briefs capturing project essence. Master visual storytelling to align with client expectations. Embrace constructive feedback, refining designs through collaboration. Transform initial ideas into polished masterpieces resonating with the audience. Engage deeply with briefs, enriching your creative process. Join this journey where every logo tells a compelling story.
