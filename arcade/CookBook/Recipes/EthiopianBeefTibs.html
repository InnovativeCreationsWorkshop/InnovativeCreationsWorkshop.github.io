ETHIOPIAN BEEF TIBS

★ ★

Medium

total time: 1 hour 25 minutes
Spiced Ethiopian-style beef with smoky skillet char, fragrant aromatics, and deep warming heat.

NOTES

  • Korerima can be substituted with cardamom if unavailable.
  • Mitmita can be replaced with a chili blend or cayenne-heavy seasoning mix.
  • An iron skillet gives the beef the best grilled flavor and crispy edges.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Beef round, sliced
    • Garlic, minced
    • Onion, sliced
    • Jalapeño, sliced
    • Poultry blend seasoning
    • Rosemary
    • Oregano
    • All-purpose seasoning
    • Salt
    • Korerima (or cardamom)
    • Mitmita (or chili blend)
    • Kibe (or butter/oil)

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Place beef in a bowl with garlic, onion, and jalapeño.
    2. Season with poultry blend, rosemary, oregano, all-purpose seasoning, salt, korerima, and mitmita.
    3. Mix thoroughly and marinate for at least 1 hour.
    4. Heat kibe in an iron skillet over medium-high heat.
    5. Cook beef until nicely grilled and slightly charred on the edges.
    6. Serve hot with injera, rice, or roti.
