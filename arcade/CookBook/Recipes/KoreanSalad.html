KBBQ SANGCHU GEOTJEORI

★

Easy

total time: 15–20 minutes
Fresh, spicy, and built to wrap grilled meat like edible origami.

    INGREDIENTS

    • Butter lettuce
    • Carrot
    • Green onion, sliced
    • Thinly sliced steak
    • Minced garlic
    • Soy sauce
    • Brown sugar
    • Rice vinegar
    • Gochugaru (Korean chili flakes)
    • Sesame oil, Sesame seeds

    DIRECTIONS

    1. In a large bowl, combine butter lettuce, shredded carrot, and green onion.
    2. In a separate bowl, mix garlic, soy sauce, brown sugar, rice vinegar, gochugaru, sesame oil, and sesame seeds.
    3. Pour sauce over vegetables and gently toss to keep lettuce crisp.
    4. Sear thin steak slices quickly on high heat until browned and slightly caramelized.
    5. Serve steak with salad and wrap in lettuce leaves to eat.
