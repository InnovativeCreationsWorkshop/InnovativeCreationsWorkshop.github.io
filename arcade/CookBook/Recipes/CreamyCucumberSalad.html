SLAVIC CREAMY CUCUMBER SALAD

★

Easy

total time: 10–15 minutes
Cool, creamy, and refreshing. A simple Slavic-style salad that pairs with just about anything.

NOTES

  • Use tomatoes on the vine, heirloom, or halved cherry tomatoes for best flavor.
  • Add fresh dill for a bright, herby finish.
  • Pair with grilled chicken or rotisserie chicken for a complete meal.
  • Add a splash of lemon juice for extra freshness.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Cucumbers (sliced into rounds or cubes)
    • Tomatoes
    • Red onion
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
    • Sour cream

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Slice cucumbers into rounds or cubes.
    2. Chop tomatoes and thinly slice red onion.
    3. Add everything to a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
    4. Add sour cream and mix well until evenly coated.
    5. Serve immediately.
