GREEK CHICKPEA SALAD

★

Easy

total time: 25–30 minutes
Bright, salty, crunchy, and packed with roasted chickpeas for extra texture and spice.

NOTES

  • Roasting the chickpeas gives the salad extra crunch and warmth.
  • Harissa seasoning and chili powder add a smoky heat that balances the fresh vegetables.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Chickpeas
    • Olive oil
    • Red wine vinegar
    • Lemon juice
    • Garlic powder
    • Dried oregano
    • Salt and black pepper
    • Red onion, diced
    • Cherry tomatoes, halved
    • Cucumber, diced
    • Mozzarella mini balls (or cheese of choice)
    • Harissa seasoning
    • Chili powder
    • Fresh parsley

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
    2. Toss chickpeas with olive oil, harissa seasoning, salt, pepper, and chili powder.
    3. Roast chickpeas until crispy, about 20 minutes.
    4. In a large bowl, combine red onion, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and mozzarella.
    5. Whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, garlic powder, oregano, and salt for the dressing.
    6. Add roasted chickpeas to the salad and pour over the dressing.
    7. Top with fresh parsley, toss everything together, and serve.
