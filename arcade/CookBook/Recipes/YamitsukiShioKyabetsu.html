YAMITSUKI SHIO KYABETSU

★

Easy

total time: 10 minutes
Crunchy, salty cabbage with late-night izakaya energy. Weirdly addictive for something this simple.

NOTES

  • Napa cabbage gives a softer texture, while regular cabbage stays extra crunchy.
  • Pairs perfectly with grilled meats, rice bowls, or anything salty and savory.


INGREDIENTS

  • Napa cabbage or regular cabbage
  • Salt
  • Garlic, grated
  • Sesame seeds
  • Ground black pepper
  • Sesame oil
  • Marmori miso paste (for serving)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Wash and chop the cabbage into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place cabbage in a large bowl and lightly salt it.
  3. Add grated garlic, sesame seeds, ground black pepper, and a splash of sesame oil.
  4. Toss everything together until evenly coated.
  5. Serve with Marmori miso paste on the side.
  6. Enjoy alongside your favorite protein.
