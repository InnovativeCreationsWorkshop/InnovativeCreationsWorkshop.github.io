UMAMI SEAWEED RICE ROLLS

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
A fusion-style seaweed rice roll inspired by sushi and kimbap — flexible, customizable, and packed with umami.

NOTES

  • Proteins: canned tuna, baked salmon, pan-seared tuna, shrimp. For kimbap style: shredded beef, pork, tamago-yaki, or tofu.
  • Let rice cool slightly before rolling so the seaweed doesn’t tear.
  • Press rice evenly for a tight roll that holds together when sliced.
  • Substitute sushi rice with Calrose rice if needed.
  • Use rice seasoning if sushi mix is unavailable.

INGREDIENTS

  • Sushi rice
  • Vinegar sushi mix
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Soy sauce
  • Protein
  • Green onion

SUGGESTED TOPPINGS

  • Carrot
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Asparagus
  • Jalapeño
  • Pickled radish
  • Pickled ginger

DIRECTIONS

  1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions.
  2. Mix in sushi seasoning while warm, then let cool slightly.
  3. Lay seaweed sheet flat on a bamboo mat or surface.
  4. Spread a thin, even layer of rice, leaving a small border.
  5. Add protein and toppings in a line across the center.
  6. Roll tightly while pressing to keep shape compact.
  7. Slice into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife.
  8. Serve with soy sauce and optional sides like miso soup or tea.
