UMAMI SEAWEED RICE ROLLS

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
A fusion-style seaweed rice roll inspired by sushi and kimbap — flexible, customizable, and packed with umami.

NOTES

  • Proteins: Canned tuna, baked salmon, pan-seared tuna, or shrimp. For Kimbap Style: KBBQ-style shredded beef, pork, tamago-yaki, tofu.
  • Let rice cool slightly before rolling so the seaweed doesn’t tear.
  • Press rice evenly for a tight roll that holds together when sliced.
  • Sub Sushi Rice with Calrose rice.
  • Sub sushi mix with rice seasoning.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Sushi Rice
    • Vinegar Sushi Mix
    • Nori - Seaweed
    • Soy sauce
    • Protein
    • Green onion

    SUGGESTED TOPPINGS

    • Carrot
    • Cucumber
    • Avocado
    • Asparagus
    • Jalapeño
    • Pickled radish
    • pickled ginger

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Cook sushi rice according to package instructions.
    2. While still warm, mix in sushi seasoning. Let rice cool slightly.
    3. Lay a sheet of seaweed flat on a clean surface or bamboo mat.
    4. Evenly press a thin layer of rice over the seaweed, leaving a small edge at the top.
    5. Add your choice of protein in a line across the center of the rice.
    6. Add suggested toppings.
    7. Roll tightly from the bottom, pressing as you go to keep it compact.
    8. Slice into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife.
    9. Serve with soy sauce and optional sides like miso soup and hot jasmine or green tea.
