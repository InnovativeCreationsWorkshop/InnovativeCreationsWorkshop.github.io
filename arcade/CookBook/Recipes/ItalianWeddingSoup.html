ITALIAN WEDDING SOUP

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
Comforting and hearty. Tender meatballs, delicate pasta, and greens in a rich, savory broth.

NOTES

  • Orzo typically cooks in about 8–10 minutes—add it accordingly so it doesn’t overcook.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Ground beef
    • Italian sausage
    • Eggs
    • Parsley (chopped)
    • Garlic powder
    • Sage
    • Breadcrumbs
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Salt, black pepper
    • Onion
    • Garlic
    • Celery
    • Carrot
    • White wine
    • Bone broth or stock
    • Orzo pasta
    • Bay leaf
    • Spinach

    DIRECTIONS

    1. In a bowl, combine ground beef, Italian sausage, eggs, chopped parsley, garlic powder, sage, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Mix well and form into bite-sized meatballs.
    2. Pan-fry the meatballs in a deep pan or pot until browned, then set aside.
    3. In the same pan, add chopped onion, garlic, celery, and carrot. Cook until softened.
    4. Deglaze with white wine, scraping up any browned bits.
    5. Add bone broth or stock and bring to a simmer. Add bay leaf.
    6. Add orzo pasta and cook for about 8–10 minutes.
    7. Return meatballs to the pot and simmer until fully cooked through.
    8. Stir in spinach at the end and cook until wilted.
    9. Garnish with Parmesan and serve hot.
