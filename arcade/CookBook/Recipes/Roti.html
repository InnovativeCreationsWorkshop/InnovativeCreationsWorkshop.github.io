ROTI

★

Easy

total time: 30–40 minutes
Soft, flaky flatbread perfect for scooping up rich curries, stews, and sauces.

NOTES

  • Boiling water helps create a softer and more pliable dough.
  • Roll the dough as thin as possible for classic layered roti texture.
  • Optional garlic butter adds extra flavor and richness.


    • INGREDIENTS

    • 2 cups flour
    • Pinch of salt
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • 2 tbsp butter or ghee
    • 1 cup boiling water
    • Extra oil for frying
    • Optional garlic butter

    DIRECTIONS

    1. In a bowl, combine flour and salt.
    2. Add oil and butter or ghee.
    3. Slowly pour in boiling water while mixing.
    4. Knead until the dough becomes soft and smooth.
    5. Roll dough into a large ball and divide into 8 equal pieces.
    6. Shape each piece into a smaller ball.
    7. Flatten and roll each ball as thin as possible.
    8. Optional: spread garlic butter over the rolled dough.
    9. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
    10. Pan fry each roti until golden spots appear on both sides.
    11. Serve warm with your favorite curries or stews.
