POLLO EN CHILE COLORADO

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
Tender chicken simmered in a smoky red chile sauce that tastes like effort, even if you took shortcuts.

NOTES

  • Serve with Spanish rice and beans of your choice. Pinto or black beans work best.
  • Using boxed rice is completely acceptable lazy-mode behavior.
  • Optional toppings: Mexican cheese blend, queso fresco, or cotija cheese.
  • If extremely lazy, substitute homemade sauce with canned red enchilada sauce.

INGREDIENTS

  • Cube chicken
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Guajillo chiles (15)
  • Ancho chile (1)
  • Chile de árbol peppers (7)
  • Tomato (1)
  • Garlic cloves (3)
  • Water (2 cups)
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Chicken bouillon (Knorr)
  • Cilantro
  • Cheese of choice

DIRECTIONS

  1. Season cubed chicken with salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  2. Brown chicken in a pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Boil chiles, tomato, and garlic until softened.
  4. Blend boiled ingredients with water, oregano, cumin, and chicken bouillon until smooth.
  5. Strain sauce into the pan with chicken.
  6. Simmer until chicken is cooked through and sauce thickens.
  7. Top with cheese and fresh cilantro before serving.
