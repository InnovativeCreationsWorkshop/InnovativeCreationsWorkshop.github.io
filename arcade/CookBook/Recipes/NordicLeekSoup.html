NORDIC LEEK & POTATO SOUP

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
A cozy, rustic soup built on buttery leeks, tender potatoes, and fresh herbs, finished with creamy richness.

NOTES

  • Protein options: grilled chicken, Italian sausage, bacon, or pancetta.
  • Add rosemary or thyme for a bolder flavor.

INGREDIENTS

  • Protein of choice
  • Butter
  • Potatoes
  • Leeks
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Bone broth
  • Water
  • Dill
  • Parsley
  • Green onion
  • Sour cream (for topping)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Brown your chosen protein in a large pot. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, melt butter and add chopped potatoes, leeks, and onion. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Sauté until softened, then add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Pour in bone broth and water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for about 15 minutes, until potatoes are tender.
  6. Stir in fresh herbs: dill, parsley, and green onion.
  7. Serve hot topped with sour cream, extra fresh herbs, and protein.
