SIMPLE FRIED RICE

★

Easy

total time: 20–30 minutes
A fast, savory stir-fry that turns rice and chicken into a full meal with minimal effort.

NOTES

  • Cold, day-old rice works best for texture and helps prevent it from becoming mushy.
  • A small splash of sesame oil at the end enhances the aroma.
  • If adding protein, refer to this recipe: Asian Style Protein Marinade →

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Protein (Optional)
    • Cooked rice
    • Onion
    • Carrot
    • Frozen peas
    • Green onion (for garnish)
    • Garlic (minced)
    • Soy sauce, oyster sauce
    • Sugar
    • Dark soy sauce (for color)
    • Black pepper
    • Egg
    • Oil or butter

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Cook your desired protein (if using) in a pan with butter or oil. Set aside.
    2. In the same pan, add more oil or butter and cook the vegetables thoroughly until softened.
    3. Scramble the egg in the same pan until just set.
    4. Add rice, then add all seasoning and sauce.
    5. Mix well over high heat, stirring vigorously to combine and heat evenly.
    6. Add the cooked protein back into the pan and mix again until fully incorporated.
    7. Garnish with green onion before serving.
