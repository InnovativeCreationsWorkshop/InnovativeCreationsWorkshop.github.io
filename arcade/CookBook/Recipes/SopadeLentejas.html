SOPA DE LENTEJAS

★

Easy

total time: 40–45 minutes
A hearty Mexican-style lentil soup built on aromatic sofrito, rich broth, and fresh toppings.

NOTES

  • Sub queso fresco with cotija or any Mexican cheese blend.

INGREDIENTS

  • Lentils (washed)
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Jalapeño
  • Roma tomatoes
  • Carrots (diced)
  • Bone broth
  • Water
  • Caldo de tomate
  • Cilantro
  • Avocado (for garnish)
  • Queso fresco

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat oil in a medium-high pot. Add garlic, onion, and jalapeño. Sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped Roma tomatoes and cook until they break down into a paste-like base.
  3. Add washed lentils and stir to combine with the base.
  4. Pour in broth and water, then add caldo de tomate seasoning. Stir well.
  5. Cover and cook for about 25 minutes over medium heat.
  6. Add diced carrots and cook for another 10 minutes until lentils and carrots are tender.
  7. Finish with fresh cilantro and stir lightly.
  8. Serve hot and garnish with avocado and queso fresco.
