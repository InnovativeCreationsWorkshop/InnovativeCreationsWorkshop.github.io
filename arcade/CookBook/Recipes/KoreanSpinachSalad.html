KOREAN SPINACH SALAD

★

Easy

total time: 10–15 minutes
Fresh, savory, and suspiciously addictive for something that started as spinach.

NOTES

  • Add sriracha or chili oil if you want heat.
  • Pairs perfectly with grilled meats, rice bowls, or eggs.
  • Best served chilled or room temperature.
  • Great side dish when you want vegetables without emotional suffering.

INGREDIENTS

  • Fresh spinach
  • Salt
  • Sesame oil
  • Spring onion
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame seeds
  • Soy sauce
  • Sriracha or chili oil (optional)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Boil the spinach for about 1 minute until bright green and softened.
  3. Drain and rinse immediately with cold water.
  4. Squeeze out as much excess water as possible.
  5. Add sesame oil, chopped spring onion, rice vinegar, sesame seeds, and soy sauce.
  6. Add sriracha or chili oil if desired.
  7. Mix well and serve as a side dish with your favorite protein.
