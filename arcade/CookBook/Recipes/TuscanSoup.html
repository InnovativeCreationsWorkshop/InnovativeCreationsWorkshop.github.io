TUSCAN WHITE BEAN & SAUSAGE SOUP

★★

Medium

total time: 40–50 minutes
A hearty, brothy bean soup with sausage and herbs.

NOTES

Add a fresh squeeze of lemon for zesty flavor.

INGREDIENTS

  • Italian sausage (crumbled)
  • Olive oil
  • Onion, chopped
  • Celery, chopped
  • Carrot, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Fresh thyme
  • Salt, Black pepper, Chili flakes
  • Tomato paste
  • White wine
  • White beans (cannellini)
  • Bone broth + water
  • Parmesan rind
  • Bay leaf
  • Spinach
  • Parmesan (for topping)
  • Cream
  • Optional- Lemon

DIRECTIONS

  1. Brown sausage in olive oil until crisp.
  2. Add onion,celery, carrot (mirepoix) and cook until soft. Stir in garlic, herbs, salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  3. Add tomato paste and cook for 1–2 minutes, then deglaze with white wine.
  4. Add beans, broth, water, bay leaf, and Parmesan rind. Simmer for ~20 minutes.
  5. Add Parm, cream, and spinach. (Optional: Fresh squeeze of lemon juice)
