RICE COOKER BOWL

★

Easy

total time: 30–40 minutes
Minimal effort, one appliance, and somehow still feels like you planned your life correctly.

NOTES

  • Extremely versatile — swap in any vegetables, protein, or sauce you like.
  • Frozen vegetables work perfectly and require zero prep energy.
  • Try adding sesame oil, sriracha, teriyaki, or chili crisp at the end.
  • Works with chicken, beef, tofu, shrimp, or whatever is available.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Rice
    • Chicken, cubed or sliced
    • Dark soy sauce
    • Mirin
    • Garlic
    • Ginger
    • Frozen peas
    • Broccoli
    • Carrots
    • Salt to taste
    • Water for rice cooker

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Marinate the chicken with dark soy sauce, mirin, garlic, and ginger.
    2. Add rice and water to the rice cooker according to package directions.
    3. Add the marinated chicken, frozen peas, broccoli, and carrots directly into the rice cooker.
    4. Add salt to taste.
    5. Close the lid and cook until the rice cooker finishes its cycle.
    6. Fluff everything together and serve hot.
