RICE COOKER BOWL

★

Easy

total time: 30–40 minutes
Minimal effort, one appliance, and somehow still feels like you planned your life correctly.

NOTES

  • Extremely versatile — swap in any vegetables, protein, or sauce.
  • Frozen vegetables work perfectly and require zero prep.
  • Try sesame oil, sriracha, teriyaki, or chili crisp at the end.
  • Works with chicken, beef, tofu, shrimp, or anything available.

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice
  • Chicken (cubed or sliced)
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Frozen peas
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Salt
  • Water (for rice cooker)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce, mirin, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Add rice and water to rice cooker.
  3. Add chicken and vegetables on top.
  4. Season lightly with salt.
  5. Cook on standard rice cycle.
  6. Fluff and serve hot.
