MINI MEATBALL PASTA

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
Tender bite-sized meatballs simmered with pasta in a creamy marinara sauce, all made in one pan for an easy comfort meal.

NOTES

  • Shell pasta, orecchiette, or cheese tortellini all work great in this recipe.
  • Use broth instead of water for extra flavor.
  • Fresh Parmesan and grated Parmesan together create the richest, creamiest sauce—use at least 1 cup of each.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Ground beef
    • Italian sausage
    • Worcestershire sauce
    • Fresh garlic, minced
    • Fresh parsley, chopped
    • Italian seasoning
    • Paprika
    • Salt & black pepper
    • Olive oil
    • Water or broth
    • Shell pasta, orecchiette, or tortellini
    • Marinara or pasta sauce
    • Heavy cream
    • Fresh Parmesan cheese
    • Grated Parmesan cheese
    • Extra parsley for garnish

    DIRECTIONS

    1. In a bowl, combine the ground beef, Italian sausage, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, parsley, Italian seasoning, paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix until just combined.
    2. Using a teaspoon, shape the mixture into small meatballs.
    3. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat and cook the meatballs until browned and almost cooked through.
    4. Add the water or broth and the pasta. Cover and cook until the pasta is tender, adding more liquid if needed.
    5. Stir in the marinara sauce and heavy cream until well combined.
    6. Add the fresh Parmesan and grated Parmesan, stirring until melted into a creamy sauce.
    7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
