KOMPOT

★

Easy

total time: 25–35 minutes
A chilled fruit drink that tastes like grandma quietly knew better than soda the entire time.

NOTES

  • Works with fresh, frozen, or dried fruit depending on what’s available.
  • Best served cold after sitting in the fridge for a few hours.
  • Adjust sugar depending on how sweet your fruit already is.
  • Peaches, apples, cherries, berries, plums, and apricots all work great.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • 6–8 cups water
    • 2–4 cups mixed fruit
    • Sugar or honey
    • 1 lemon slice (optional)
    • Cinnamon stick (optional)
    • Fresh mint (optional)
    • Ice for serving

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Add water to a large pot and bring to a gentle boil.
    2. Add the fruit and simmer for 15–25 minutes until softened and flavorful.
    3. Stir in sugar or honey to taste.
    4. Add optional lemon or cinnamon for extra flavor.
    5. Remove from heat and let cool.
    6. Serve warm or chill in the refrigerator and serve cold over ice.
