RED BEANS & RICE

★★★

Medium Hard

total time: ~1.5–2 hours
This classic Cajun southern comfort dish brings together slow-simmered red beans, smoky spices, and tender rice for a hearty, soulful meal.

NOTES

  • Garnish with green onions and dried parsley.
  • If using dried beans, soak them overnight. If using canned beans, reduce cook time significantly.
  • You can substitute bell pepper for poblano pepper if needed.
  • Use Slap Ya Mama seasoning instead of salt.
  • For a crockpot version, cook on high for about 4 hours.
  • Optional: add a turkey leg for extra richness and flavor.

INGREDIENTS

  • Rice
  • Sausage
  • Holy Trinity (celery, bell peppers, onion)
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Red beans
  • Bone broth
  • Bay leaf
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Oregano
  • Thyme
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Red chili flakes
  • Chicken bouillon

DIRECTIONS

  1. Brown sausage in a large pot, then remove and set aside.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in butter until soft.
  3. Add celery and peppers (Holy Trinity) and cook until softened.
  4. Stir in flour and cook briefly to form a base.
  5. Add spices and mix well.
  6. Pour in bone broth and add bay leaf. Bring to a boil.
  7. Return sausage and add red beans.
  8. Reduce heat and simmer 1–1.5 hours until beans are tender.
  9. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  10. Serve over rice and garnish with green onions and parsley.
