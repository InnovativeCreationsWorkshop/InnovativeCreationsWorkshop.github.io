STREET TACOS

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
Messy hands, dripping salsa, zero regrets. Backyard taqueria energy without the parking lot line.

NOTES

  • Add Mexican-style cheese for extra richness and taco shop vibes.
  • Char the salsa ingredients hard — the blackened edges build flavor.

INGREDIENTS

  • Choice of protein
  • Carne asada
  • Orange juice
  • Lime juice
  • Onion
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Taco seasoning
  • Corn tortillas
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Mexican-style cheese (optional)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Marinate carne asada with orange juice, lime juice, onion, cilantro, and seasonings.
  2. Let sit at least 20 minutes (longer improves flavor).
  3. Cook meat in a hot pan or grill until browned and fully cooked.
  4. Chop into taco-sized pieces.
  5. Finely chop fresh onion and cilantro for topping.
  6. Char jalapeños and tomatoes in a pan until blackened for salsa.
  7. Blend charred vegetables with onion, lemon juice, and salt until smooth.
  8. Warm corn tortillas.
  9. Assemble tacos with meat, onion, cilantro, salsa, and optional cheese.
