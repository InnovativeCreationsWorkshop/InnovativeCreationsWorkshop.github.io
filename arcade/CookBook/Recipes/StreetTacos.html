STREET TACOS

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
Messy hands, dripping salsa, zero regrets. Backyard taqueria energy without the parking lot line.

NOTES

  • Add Mexican-style cheese for extra richness and taco shop vibes.
  • Char the salsa ingredients hard — the blackened edges build the flavor.


INGREDIENTS

  • Choice of protein
  • Carne asada
  • Orange juice
  • Lime juice
  • Onion
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Taco seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Chili powder
  • Cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Corn tortillas
  • Mexican-style cheese (optional)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Marinate the carne asada with orange juice, lime juice, onion, cilantro, taco seasoning, salt, black pepper, oregano, cumin, chili powder, and cayenne if using.
  2. Let the meat marinate for at least 20 minutes or longer for deeper flavor.
  3. Cook the protein in a hot pan or grill until browned and fully cooked.
  4. Chop the cooked meat into small taco-sized pieces.
  5. Finely chop fresh onion and cilantro for topping.
  6. For the salsa, pan fry jalapeños and tomatoes until blackened.
  7. Add the charred vegetables to a blender with onion, lemon juice, and salt. Blend until smooth.
  8. Warm the corn tortillas.
  9. Assemble tacos with meat, onion, cilantro, salsa, and optional Mexican-style cheese.
