CHICKEN TINGA TOSTADAS

★

Easy

total time: 20–25 minutes
Smoky, saucy shredded chicken layered over crispy tostadas with creamy toppings and plenty of texture. Also works as a scoopable party-style platter with tortilla chips.

NOTES

  • Rotisserie chicken works best for quick prep and extra flavor.
  • Adding red enchilada sauce creates a richer, saucier tinga.
  • Customize toppings with avocado, jalapeños, or extra cheese.
  • For a chip version, layer ingredients on a platter and scoop with tortilla chips instead of building individual tostadas.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, diced
    • Shredded rotisserie chicken
    • Salsa
    • Red enchilada sauce
    • Cotija cheese
    • Refried beans
    • Tostada shells or tortilla chips
    • Crema Mexicana or sour cream
    • Shredded lettuce
    • Red onion
    • Cilantro

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add diced onion and cook until softened.
    3. Stir in shredded chicken and salsa.
    4. Add red enchilada sauce and cook for about 10 minutes.
    5. Mix in cotija cheese until incorporated into the chicken mixture.
    6. Spread a layer of refried beans onto each tostada shell.
    7. Drizzle with crema or sour cream.
    8. Top with the chicken tinga mixture.
    9. Finish with lettuce, salsa, red onion, cotija cheese, cilantro, and any additional toppings.
    10. Serve immediately.
