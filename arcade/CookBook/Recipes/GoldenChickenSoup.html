GOLDEN CHICKEN SOUP

★

Easy

stovetop: 2 hours | crockpot: ~4 hours
A slow, golden broth for sick days, tired days, or rest days.

NOTES

  • If using a crockpot, cook on high for about 4 hours and check moisture levels periodically, adding water as needed.
  • For a richer flavor, add a splash of soy sauce and sesame oil.
  • Ginger level controls the “healing intensity” of the dish.
  • Goji berries add subtle sweetness and a restorative depth.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Whole or half chicken (bone-in preferred)
    • Carrot, sliced
    • Ginger, sliced
    • Garlic, smashed
    • Onion, julienne
    • Napa cabbage, chopped
    • Goji berries
    • Green Onion
    • Bone broth
    • Water (as needed)

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Add all ingredients to a large pot all at once.
    2. Pour in 1 cup bone broth and 1 cup water.
    3. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook on low for 2 hours until chicken is tender and falling apart.
    4. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper to taste.
    5. Garnish with fresh green onion.
