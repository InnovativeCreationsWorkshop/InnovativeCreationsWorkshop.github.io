JAPANESE CURRY

★

Easy

total time: 35–45 minutes
Comforting Japanese-style curry with tender vegetables, rich broth, and your choice of protein served over warm rice.

NOTES

  • Chicken or Beef works best for this.
  • Jalapeño adds a subtle heat if you want a spicy twist on the classic curry.
  • Calrose rice gives the most authentic texture, but jasmine rice works beautifully too.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Potatoes, cubed
    • Carrots, cubed
    • Onion, chopped
    • Choice of protein
    • Jalapeño (optional)
    • Bone broth
    • Water
    • Japanese curry mix cubes
    • Calrose rice or jasmine rice

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Cube the potatoes, carrots, and onion. Prepare your choice of protein into bite-sized pieces.
    2. Add the vegetables, protein, optional jalapeño, bone broth, and water into a large pot.
    3. Bring to a boil and cook until the potatoes are mostly tender.
    4. Add the Japanese curry mix cubes and stir well until fully dissolved. Follow package directions for quantity and thickness.
    5. Simmer for a few more minutes until the curry thickens and the vegetables are fully cooked.
    6. Serve hot over Calrose rice or jasmine rice.
