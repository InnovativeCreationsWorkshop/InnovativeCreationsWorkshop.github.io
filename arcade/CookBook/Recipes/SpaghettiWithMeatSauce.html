SPAGHETTI WITH MEAT SAUCE

★

Easy

total time: 30 minutes
Classic comfort pasta with rich meat sauce and melty cheese energy.

NOTES

  • Optional add-ins: onion, garlic, and mushrooms for extra flavor.
  • Top with your favorite cheese like parmesan or mozzarella.


    • INGREDIENTS

    • Spaghetti
    • Ground beef
    • Oil
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
    • Italian seasoning
    • Pasta sauce (ex: Prego traditional)
    • Pasta water
    • Parmesan or mozzarella cheese

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
    2. Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat.
    3. Cook ground beef with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning until browned.
    4. Add optional onion, garlic, or mushrooms if using.
    5. Pour in your favorite pasta sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
    6. Add a splash of pasta water to thicken and enrich the sauce.
    7. Drain the pasta well and toss into the sauce until coated.
    8. Top with parmesan or mozzarella cheese and serve.
