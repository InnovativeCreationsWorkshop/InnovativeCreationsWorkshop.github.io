SPAGHETTI WITH MEAT SAUCE

★

Easy

total time: 30 minutes
Classic comfort pasta with rich meat sauce and melty cheese energy.

NOTES

  • Optional add-ins: onion, garlic, and mushrooms for extra flavor.
  • Top with parmesan, mozzarella, or your favorite cheese.

INGREDIENTS

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Pasta sauce (e.g. Prego Traditional)
  • Pasta water
  • Parmesan or mozzarella cheese

DIRECTIONS

  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook ground beef with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning until browned.
  4. Add optional onion, garlic, or mushrooms if using.
  5. Pour in pasta sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Add a splash of pasta water to enrich and thicken the sauce.
  7. Toss cooked pasta into the sauce until fully coated.
  8. Top with cheese and serve hot.
