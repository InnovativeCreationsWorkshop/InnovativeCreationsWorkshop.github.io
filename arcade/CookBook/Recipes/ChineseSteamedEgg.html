CHINESE STEAMED EGG

★

Easy

total time: 20 minutes
Silky, savory steamed eggs with comforting texture and simple flavors.

NOTES

  • Eat with rice or enjoy by itself.
  • Optional toppings: nori, bonito flakes, or chili oil for spice.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • 3 eggs, beaten
    • Warm water
    • Soy sauce
    • Sesame oil
    • Scallions, chopped

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
    2. Add warm water and mix gently.
    3. Place the bowl into a steam basket.
    4. Steam for about 15 minutes until set.
    5. Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil.
    6. Top with chopped scallions and serve.
