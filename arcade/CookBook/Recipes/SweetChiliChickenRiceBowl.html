SWEET CHILI CHICKEN RICE BOWL

★★

Medium

total time: 30–40 minutes
A sweet, spicy, and tangy wok-style chicken bowl with crisp vegetables and a glossy chili glaze.

NOTES

  • If lazy, use store-bought kung pao or sweet & sour sauce instead of making sauce from scratch.
  • Do not overuse sauce—start small and adjust as needed.
  • For a low-carb option, replace rice with shredded cabbage.
  • Add lime at the end for extra brightness (especially if not using store-bought sauce).

INGREDIENTS

  • Jasmine rice (or shredded cabbage for low-carb option)
  • Chicken breast, diced
  • Green beans or snap peas
  • Onion, sliced
  • Garlic powder
  • Chili pepper
  • Cornstarch (optional)
  • Sweet soy glaze
  • Sweet Thai chili sauce
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Lime

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat a wok over high heat and add a little oil.
  2. Add diced chicken and season with garlic powder, chili pepper, and onion.
  3. Cook until chicken is browned and nearly cooked through.
  4. Add sweet soy glaze and/or sweet Thai chili sauce (skip cornstarch if using store-bought sauce).
  5. Toss in green beans or snap peas and stir-fry.
  6. Cook for about 10 minutes or until vegetables reach desired tenderness and sauce thickens.
  7. Serve over jasmine rice or shredded cabbage for a low-carb option.
  8. Garnish with sesame seeds, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
