BURRITO

★

Easy

total time: 25–35 minutes
One tortilla carrying the emotional weight of an entire meal prep strategy.

NOTES

  • Make a batch and freeze for up to 1–2 weeks for easy meals.
  • If freezing, skip salsa and sour cream before reheating — the texture gets weird fast.
  • Wrap burritos tightly in parchment paper, then store in a zip-top freezer bag.


INGREDIENTS

  • Burrito-size flour tortillas
  • Spanish rice
  • Pinto beans or black beans
  • Cheese
  • Protein of choice
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Fresh cilantro

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prepare Spanish rice according to your preferred method.
  2. Cook your protein of choice.
  3. Warm the beans and tortillas.
  4. Layer rice, beans, cheese, protein, salsa, sour cream, and fresh cilantro onto each tortilla.
  5. Fold in the sides and roll tightly into a burrito.
  6. Enjoy fresh, or prepare for freezing.
  7. To freeze, leave out salsa and sour cream. Wrap burritos in parchment paper and place into a zip-top freezer bag.
