CREAMY GARLIC MUSHROOM SOUP

★★

Medium

total time: 35–45 minutes
Earthy mushrooms, layered spices, and a silky finish that tastes like comfort with depth.

NOTES

The fried mushroom topping adds texture and a restaurant-style finish.

INGREDIENTS

  • Mushrooms
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Oregano
  • Fresh thyme
  • Vegeta or all-purpose seasoning
  • White wine
  • Bone broth
  • Water
  • Cornstarch
  • Milk or Cream
  • Parsley (chopped)
  • Optional: sour cream or extra cream

DIRECTIONS

  1. Brown mushrooms in a pan until deeply golden. Set some aside for topping.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in butter until soft and fragrant.
  3. Add mushrooms back in and stir to coat in the butter base.
  4. Mix in paprika, cayenne, oregano, thyme, and seasoning blend.
  5. Pour in white wine to deglaze the pan and simmer until reduced.
  6. Add bone broth and water, then bring to a gentle simmer.
  7. Whisk cornstarch with milk or cream, then slowly stir into the soup.
  8. Simmer until thick and silky.
  9. Adjust seasoning and add extra cream or sour cream if desired.
  10. Serve topped with parsley and reserved mushrooms.
