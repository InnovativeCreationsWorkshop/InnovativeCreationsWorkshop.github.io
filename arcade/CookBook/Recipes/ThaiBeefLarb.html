THAI BEEF LARB

★

Easy

total time: 15–20 minutes
Savory, bright, and herb-packed. A quick Thai-inspired dish with bold flavor and fresh crunch.

NOTES

  • Add sriracha or chili paste on top for extra heat.
  • Adjust sweetness to taste for balance.
  • Serve with butter lettuce cups for a fresh, wrap-style bite.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Ground beef
    • Sugar
    • Fish sauce
    • Lime juice
    • Mirin
    • Shallot (julienned)
    • Thai chilies
    • Cilantro
    • Mint
    • Butter lettuce (for serving)

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Cook ground beef in a pan over medium-high heat.
    2. Add sugar, fish sauce, lime juice, and mirin. Cook until the beef is browned and fully cooked.
    3. Turn off the heat.
    4. Add julienned shallot, Thai chilies, cilantro, and mint.
    5. Mix until the herbs are slightly wilted from the heat.
    6. Serve in butter lettuce cups, taco-style.
