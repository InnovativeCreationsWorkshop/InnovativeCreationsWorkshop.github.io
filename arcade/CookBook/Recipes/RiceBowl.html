RICE BOWL

★

Easy

total time: 20–30 minutes
A customizable rice bowl built around sushi rice and your choice of toppings, proteins, and sauces for a quick, flexible meal.

NOTES

  • Works great with calrose or sushi rice.
  • Can be eaten as a bowl or wrapped into small rice rolls using seaweed snacks.
  • Mix and match proteins and toppings based on what you have available.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Calrose or sushi rice
    • Cucumber (diced or shredded)
    • Green onion, sliced
    • Seaweed snacks
    • Kewpie mayo
    • Sriracha
    • Rice seasoning (furikake or similar)
    • Protein of choice: canned tuna, canned eel, shrimp, chicken, beef, tofu, salmon, or tuna steak
    • Soy sauce (optional)
    • Sesame seeds (optional)

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Cook the calrose or sushi rice according to package instructions and let it steam until fluffy.
    2. Prepare your chosen protein (or use it ready-to-eat if canned).
    3. Slice or shred cucumber and chop green onions.
    4. Assemble the bowl with rice as the base, then layer on protein and toppings.
    5. Sprinkle with rice seasoning and drizzle with kewpie mayo and sriracha.
    6. Serve as a bowl or wrap portions into seaweed snacks to make small rice rolls.
