CAPRESE SALAD

★

Easy

total time: 10 minutes
Bright, fresh, and aggressively summer-coded. Minimal effort, maximum table flex.

NOTES

  • Use ripe cherry tomatoes for the best flavor balance.
  • Finish with balsamic glaze right before serving so everything stays fresh and glossy.


INGREDIENTS

  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper

DIRECTIONS

  1. Slice or tear fresh mozzarella into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Cut cherry tomatoes in half.
  3. Arrange mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil leaves on a serving plate or bowl.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and a small amount of balsamic vinegar.
  5. Season with salt and ground black pepper.
  6. Finish with balsamic glaze and serve immediately.
