ASIAN STYLE PROTEIN MARINADE

★

Easy

prep time: 15 minutes
A versatile marinade that works with chicken, shrimp, beef, or pork for multiple Asian-style dishes.

NOTES

  • This base works across multiple cooking styles, so think of it as a flavor foundation rather than a strict recipe.
  • For spice add : Chili oil or sriracha or chili paste or gochugaru or gochujang depending on the dish.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Soy sauce
    • Oyster sauce
    • Sugar
    • Fish sauce (or mushroom sauce)
    • Mirin
    • Garlic (minced)
    • Sesame oil
    • For heat see notes.
    • Protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, beef, pork)

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl until fully combined.
    2. Add protein and coat evenly in the marinade.
    3. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, up to 12 hours (overnight for deeper flavor).
    4. Cook protein using your desired method or recipe.

    USES

    • Korean BBQ (KBBQ)
    • Fried rice
    • Stir-fry noodles
    • Lettuce wraps
    • Rice bowls
    • Grilled skewers
    • Ramen topping
