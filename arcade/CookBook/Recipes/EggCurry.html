EGG CURRY

★

Easy

total time: 35–40 minutes
Boiled eggs simmered in a rich onion-tomato masala with warming spices and comforting curry flavor.

NOTES

  • Frying the eggs with turmeric and chili powder adds extra flavor and color to the curry.
  • Enjoy with roti, naan, rice, or paratha for a full comforting meal.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Eggs
    • Red onions, chopped
    • Tomatoes, chopped
    • Garlic
    • Ginger
    • Green chilies
    • Oil
    • Cumin seeds
    • Red chili powder
    • Turmeric
    • Coriander powder
    • Cumin powder
    • Salt
    • Fresh cilantro
    • Garam masala
    • Fenugreek

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Boil the eggs for 6–7 minutes. Peel and set aside.
    2. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds. Add chopped onions and cook until caramelized.
    3. Add tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Add a splash of water, cover, and cook until the tomatoes soften.
    4. Let the mixture cool slightly, then blend until smooth.
    5. In the same pan, fry the boiled eggs with turmeric and red chili powder until lightly golden. Remove and set aside.
    6. Add the blended masala back into the pan. Stir in red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Cook until thick and paste-like.
    7. Add warm water to create the curry consistency you like. Season with salt.
    8. Finish with fresh cilantro, garam masala, fenugreek, and the fried eggs. Simmer briefly before serving.
