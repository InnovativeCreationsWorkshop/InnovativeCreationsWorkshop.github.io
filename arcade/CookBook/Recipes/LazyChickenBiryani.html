LAZY CHICKEN BIRYANI

★

Easy

total time: 45–60 minutes
Minimal effort, big flavor. A shortcut biryani made right in the rice cooker.

NOTES

  • Use bone broth with water for a richer, deeper flavor.
  • If the rice turns out too watery, leave it in the rice cooker for an extra 10–15 minutes.
  • Marinate longer for more tender, flavorful chicken.
  • Add dried or fresh curry leaves for more flavor.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Chicken (cubed)
    • Salt
    • Turmeric
    • Biryani spice mix
    • Garlic (minced)
    • Chili
    • Yogurt
    • Leek (chopped)
    • Vegetable oil
    • Rice (washed)
    • Water or broth

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Marinate cubed chicken with salt, turmeric, biryani spice mix, garlic, chili, and yogurt. Let sit for at least 30 minutes (longer for better flavor and tenderness).
    2. Chop the leek.
    3. In a rice cooker, add vegetable oil, chopped leek, washed rice, and water or broth.
    4. Layer the marinated chicken on top of the rice.
    5. Cook using the standard rice setting.
    6. Let rest for a few minutes, then mix before serving.
