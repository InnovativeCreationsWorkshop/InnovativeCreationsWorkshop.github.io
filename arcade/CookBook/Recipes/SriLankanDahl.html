SRI LANKAN DAHL

★

Easy

total time: 30–40 minutes
Comforting lentils simmered with warm spices and coconut milk. Creamy, fragrant, and built for rice.

NOTES

  • Drain the lentils for a thicker texture, or leave some liquid for a smoother dahl.
  • Adjust simmering time depending on how creamy or chunky you want the final result.
  • Works well as either a main dish or a side.

INGREDIENTS

  • Red lentils
  • Salt
  • Turmeric
  • Onion (sliced)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Dahl masala
  • Coconut milk
  • Rice (for serving)

DIRECTIONS

  1. Wash the red lentils thoroughly until the water runs mostly clear.
  2. Boil the lentils like rice. Halfway through cooking, add salt and turmeric.
  3. Cook until just tender, then drain slightly if you prefer a thicker dahl.
  4. In a pan, fry onion and mustard seeds until aromatic.
  5. Add turmeric, salt, and dahl masala. Stir into a fragrant paste.
  6. Add cooked lentils and simmer for 5–10 minutes depending on desired texture.
  7. Pour in coconut milk, mix well, and simmer until creamy.
  8. Serve hot with rice.
