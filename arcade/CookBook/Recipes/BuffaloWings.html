BUFFALO WINGS

★

Easy

total time: 1 hour 15 minutes–2 hours
Crispy, tangy buffalo wings with a buttery RedHot finish. Great for game day, movie night, or whenever spicy comfort food sounds right.

NOTES

  • For saucier wings, keep them covered for the full bake time.
  • For crispier wings with less sauce retention, uncover halfway through cooking.
  • Flip the wings during baking for even cooking and browning.
  • Serve with Caesar salad and ranch dressing for dipping.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Chicken wings
    • Water
    • Vinegar
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
    • Garlic powder
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • Frank's RedHot Sauce
    • Ranch dressing (for serving)
    • Caesar salad (optional side)

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Soak the wings in water and vinegar for at least 1 hour.
    2. Pat the wings dry thoroughly.
    3. Season with salt, black pepper, and garlic powder.
    4. Place wings on a baking sheet and bake covered at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
    5. For less sauce and more crispiness, uncover halfway through cooking. Flip the wings during baking.
    6. Remove from the oven and toss with butter and RedHot Sauce until evenly coated.
    7. Return to the oven and broil for about 15 minutes, or until crispy and nicely colored.
    8. Serve hot with ranch dressing and your favorite side.
