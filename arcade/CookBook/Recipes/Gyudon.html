GYUDON

★

Easy

total time: 20–25 minutes
A classic Japanese beef rice bowl featuring tender sliced beef simmered in a sweet and savory sauce over fluffy rice.

NOTES

  • For extra heat, sauté sliced Thai chilies with the onions or drizzle sriracha over the finished bowl.
  • Thinly sliced ribeye or shaved beef works best for tender, flavorful bites.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Thinly sliced beef
    • Yellow onion, sliced
    • Soy sauce
    • Cooking sake
    • Mirin
    • Sugar
    • Hondashi
    • Garlic paste
    • Ginger paste
    • Water
    • Cooked white rice
    • Scallions, sliced

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Sauté the sliced onion in a pan over medium heat until softened.
    2. Add the thinly sliced beef and cook until lightly browned.
    3. Stir in the soy sauce, cooking sake, mirin, sugar, hondashi, garlic paste, ginger paste, and a splash of water.
    4. Simmer until the sauce reduces slightly and coats the beef and onions.
    5. Serve the beef mixture over a bowl of hot cooked rice.
    6. Garnish with sliced scallions and add sriracha if desired.
