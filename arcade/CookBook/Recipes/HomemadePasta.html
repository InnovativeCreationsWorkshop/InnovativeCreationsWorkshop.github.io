HOMEMADE PASTA

★★

Medium

total time: 1 hour
Fresh pasta with simple ingredients and a little arm workout built in.

NOTES

  • 100 grams of all-purpose flour is about 3/4 cup.
  • The dough should feel smooth and spring back when pressed.
  • Roll the pasta very thin — almost translucent — for the best texture.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 3 eggs

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Place the flour onto a clean flat surface and form it into a mound.
    2. Create a well in the center like a small cup shape.
    3. Add the eggs into the center and whisk with a fork.
    4. Slowly pull flour into the eggs until the center becomes pasty.
    5. Use your hands to combine everything into a dough.
    6. Knead vigorously for about 10 minutes until smooth.
    7. Press the dough lightly — it should spring back.
    8. Wrap the dough in a cloth and rest for 30 minutes.
    9. Use a rolling pin to flatten and stretch the dough very thin.
    10. Roll the sheet of pasta, then slice into strips.
    11. Boil in salted water until cooked through and tender.
