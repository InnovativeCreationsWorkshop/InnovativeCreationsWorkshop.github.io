BIG MAC SALAD

★

Easy

total time: 20–25 minutes
All the burger joy, none of the carb scaffolding. A forkable backyard cookout illusion.

NOTES

  • Add mustard for full burger illusion mode. Toasted sesame seeds give subtle bun vibes.
  • Don’t over-stir the beef if you want crispy edges. Perfect for when you want fast food energy with slightly better decisions.

    • INGREDIENTS

    • Iceberg Lettuce
    • Ground beef
    • Salt, Black pepper
    • Garlic (minced or powder)
    • White onion, chopped
    • Tomatoes, chopped
    • Pickles, chopped
    • Cheese (shredded or crumbled)
    • Thousand Island dressing

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Cook the beef in a pan over medium-high heat. Season with salt, pepper, and garlic. Brown until slightly crispy and crumbly.
    2. In a large bowl, combine lettuce to build the crunchy base.
    3. Add chopped tomatoes, onion, pickles, and cheese over the lettuce.
    4. Spoon hot ground beef over the top, letting it slightly wilt the greens.
    5. Drizzle with Thousand Island dressing and toss lightly or leave layered.
