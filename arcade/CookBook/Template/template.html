BIG MAC SALAD

★

Easy

total time: 20–25 minutes
All the burger joy, none of the carb scaffolding. A forkable backyard cookout illusion.

NOTES

  • Add mustard for full burger illusion mode. Toasted sesame seeds give subtle bun vibes.
  • Don’t over-stir the beef if you want crispy edges. Perfect for fast food energy with slightly better decisions.


    • INGREDIENTS

    • Iceberg Lettuce
    • Ground beef
    • Salt, Black pepper
    • Garlic (minced or powder)
    • White onion, chopped
    • Tomatoes, chopped
    • Pickles, chopped
    • Cheese (shredded or crumbled)
    • Thousand Island dressing

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Cook beef in a pan over medium-high heat. Season and brown until slightly crispy.
    2. Place lettuce in a large bowl.
    3. Add tomatoes, onion, pickles, and cheese.
    4. Add hot beef on top.
    5. Drizzle with Thousand Island dressing and serve.
